ClassPartHor

A tool for speakers to interact with their audience and keep a record of these interactions.

The participant can indicate their interest to share or to respond to the speaker's question by hitting a button on the application. The speaker is notified in real-time via his device and can easily select from the list of participants, which can be sorted real-time by the number of times each participant has previously shared.
